Security Verification Guide

Dear Valued Customer,

At TrustBridge Bank, we are dedicated to protecting your financial security. This guide explains our comprehensive security verification process to safeguard your account against potential fraudulent activities.

Why We Trigger Security Alerts

Our advanced monitoring systems detect unusual account activities, including:

  • Transactions from unfamiliar locations
  • Unexpected large purchases
  • Logins from new devices
  • Suspicious account access attempts
  • Unusual spending patterns

Our Verification Process

  1. Immediate Notification

    We alert you instantly via text message, email, and mobile banking app notification with transaction details.

  2. Verification Methods
    • Online Verification: Confirm or dispute transactions through online banking
    • Phone Verification: Call our security hotline for guided verification
    • Mobile App Verification: Use biometric authentication to confirm transactions
  3. Additional Security Steps

    We may require you to:

    • Answer personalized security questions
    • Confirm recent legitimate transactions
    • Provide proof of current location
